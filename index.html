Instructions

Controls

A Move Left
D Move Right
Space Jump
F Throw

How to play?

Basic progression:

  • Kill enemies by either throwing bottles or jumping on them
  • You start with 5 bottles, more can be picked up in the game
  • Each time a enemy hits you, you lose health

How to win?

  • All enemies must be defeated
  • The boss must be defeated

When do i lose?

  • If your health drops to 0
  • You do not manage to defeat all enemies before they leave the map

Other important info:

  • Coins currently do not serve a purpose in the game
  • Try to "jump-kill" as many enemies as possible as bottles are rare
